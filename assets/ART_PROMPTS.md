# Generated Art Prompts

These bitmap assets are original generated art for this project. They avoid official MapleStory, Nexon, logo, UI, or character assets.

## Backgrounds

- `backgrounds/meadow-bg.png`: bright maple meadow side-scroller background, whimsical mushroom village hints, autumn leaves, soft mountains, painterly 2D MMORPG mood.
- `backgrounds/forest-bg.png`: enchanted mushroom forest side-scroller background, layered trees, glowing spores, readable path, painterly fantasy platformer mood.
- `backgrounds/shrine-bg.png`: mushroom king shrine side-scroller background, moonlit maple ruins, stone arch, warm lantern glow, boss-stage mood.
- `backgrounds/cave-bg.png`: dark cave side-scroller background, stalactites, layered rock arches, underground city silhouettes, lantern glow.
- `backgrounds/snow-bg.png`: snowy mountain field side-scroller background, icy cliffs, blue-white mountains, aurora sky, crystal formations.
- `backgrounds/lava-bg.png`: volcanic lava cavern side-scroller background, basalt cliffs, magma rivers, ember-lit rock arches, dragon-nest mood.
- `backgrounds/castle-bg.png`: ruined dark fantasy castle side-scroller background, gothic arches, purple-black stone walls, moonlit final-stage mood.

## Actor Atlas

- `sprites/actors-raw.png`: 3x2 magenta-background atlas containing an original chibi adventurer, blue snail, green slime, red mushroom, mossy golem, and crowned mushroom boss.
- `sprites/actors.png`: chroma-keyed transparent version used by the game renderer.

## Monster Sprites

- `sprites/mob-source-sheet.png`: 4x3 magenta-background source sheet with original right-facing monster sprites.
- `sprites/mob_blue_snail.png`, `sprites/mob_zombie_mushroom.png`, `sprites/mob_stump.png`, `sprites/mob_bubbling.png`, `sprites/mob_goblin.png`, `sprites/mob_mummy.png`, `sprites/mob_yeti.png`, `sprites/mob_drake.png`, `sprites/mob_darklord.png`, `sprites/mob_pig.png`, `sprites/mob_bat.png`, `sprites/mob_eye.png`: chroma-keyed transparent single-file monsters. Each sprite is original art, faces right, and is bottom-centered for the game renderer.
